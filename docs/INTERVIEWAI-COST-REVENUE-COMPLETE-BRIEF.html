InterviewAI / Aria — Cost, revenue & profit (complete briefing)

Document for: co-founder, advisor, or investor
Version: 1.0 · Date: 9 April 2026
Currency: £ GBP (OpenAI and some vendors bill in USD; planning often uses £1 = $1.25 unless stated)

Executive summary

  • Product: AI interview preparation — mocks (CV + job description), grading, Aria Live (voice + coach), CV tools, academy, etc.
  • Stack cost: Vercel (app + API), Supabase (auth + database), OpenAI (LLM + TTS). Stripe when card payments are live.
  • Largest variable cost at scale: OpenAI — especially long Aria Live. A standard mock (questions + grade) is cheap per completion (order of pennies of AI in this model).
  • Revenue (as in the app): Pro £12/month or £99/year; Free tier: 3 mock interviews per month (UTC). Card checkout: wire Stripe; Pro can be toggled in Supabase (user_profiles.is_pro) for tests.
  • Profit per Pro (contribution, after variable costs + card fees): often ~£7–10+/month for a typical user; ~£0–3 for a heavy Aria user; can be loss-making if someone abuses unlimited — use fair use or Pro+ for flagship live voice.
  • This is a planning model, not a tax quote. Replace numbers with real OpenAI, Vercel, Supabase, and Stripe data.

Gross £12 → fees (often ~2.5% + 20p on many UK cards) order of ~40p → net ≈ £11.20–11.60 (round £11.30 below; confirm in Stripe).

4. Future: “real” live interviewer

Today: HTTP coach + TTS; not full flagship speech-to-speech. Realtime / long audio = separate P&L — price, minute bundles, or Pro+. See docs/INVESTOR-COST-REAL-INTERVIEWER-MODE.md in the repo for detail.

5. Commercial levers

  1. Fair use / soft caps on TTS or long Aria for Pro if needed.
  2. Pro+ for unlimited or realtime “hiring manager” mode.
  3. B2B for higher £/seat later.
  4. Track weekly: OpenAI spend; £ or $ per active Pro; Aria minutes or TTS volume per Pro.

6. Metrics checklist

  • MRR, active Pro, churn
  • OpenAI bill (total and per active user)
  • Mocks per user; Aria minutes or TTS per Pro
  • Gross vs net after Stripe
